Our Process

How We Work

A structured, transparent consulting process from initial discovery to final handover — ensuring quality at every stage.

Discover

Structured discovery session to understand your objectives, existing data assets, technical environment, and project scope. We define what success looks like before a single dataset is touched.

Data Audit

Comprehensive assessment of existing spatial data: format quality, coordinate systems, topology, completeness, and gaps. Produces a data readiness report and migration/conversion plan.

Spatial Architecture

Geodatabase schema design, layer hierarchy, relationship classes, projection standards, enterprise topology rules, and naming conventions. This is the blueprint for your entire GIS system.

GIS / BIM / Remote Sensing Processing

Core technical delivery: data conversion, BIM integration, image analysis, model building, QA/QC pipeline execution, and geodatabase population. All processing is documented and reproducible.

QA / QC

Automated and manual quality assurance: topology checks, attribute validation, projection verification, spatial integrity checks, and compliance reporting. Nothing leaves without passing our standards checklist.

Dashboard & Web Platform

Deployment of operational dashboards, Experience Builder apps, StoryMaps, or custom web GIS portals. User access configuration, role-based permissions, and performance optimization for stakeholder-facing delivery.

Training & Handover

Structured knowledge transfer: user training sessions, technical documentation, operational guides, and ongoing advisory support. Your team is empowered to own and extend the system independently.
